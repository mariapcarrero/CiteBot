# Onboarding FAQ

## When do I get my laptop?
The IT team hands over the laptop on the first day of work, during the morning
induction session.

## How do I request time off?
Time off is requested through the HR portal at least 10 days in advance. The direct
manager must approve the request.

## What is the probation period?
The probation period is 3 months for all new hires, with no exceptions based on
seniority.

## How do I access health benefits?
Health benefits activate automatically starting on day 30 of employment. No
additional request is required.
