# Company Travel Policy

## Approval
Every business trip must be approved by the direct manager at least 5 business days
in advance. International trips also require approval from the area director.

## Reimbursements
Travel expenses (transportation, lodging, and meals) are reimbursed within 15
business days after submitting receipts. The daily meal limit is 40 USD for domestic
trips and 60 USD for international trips.

## Lodging
Employees should book accommodations up to a 4-star category. Any exception must be
approved in advance by the area director.

## Transportation
Economy class is prioritized for flights under 6 hours. For flights longer than 6
hours, premium economy class is authorized.
