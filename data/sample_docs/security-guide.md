# Basic Information Security Guide

## Passwords
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, combine uppercase, lowercase,
numbers, and symbols, and be renewed every 90 days.

## Two-Factor Authentication
Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all systems that handle customer
data.

## Personal Devices
Confidential company information may not be stored on personal devices that are not
enrolled in the mobile device management (MDM) program.

## Incident Reporting
Any suspected phishing attempt or security incident must be reported within one hour
of detection by emailing security@company.example.
